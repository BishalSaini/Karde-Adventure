Our Services

Explore our exciting range of water sports activities

Jet Ski Thrill

Feel the speed and excitement as you ride the waves on a powerful Jet Ski. Perfect for thrill seekers and adventure lovers! Experience the rush of adrenaline as you navigate through the pristine waters of Karde Beach with professional guidance.

Kayaking

Explore the calm waters of Karde Beach at your own pace. A peaceful experience perfect for nature lovers, photographers, and those seeking tranquility. Paddle through pristine waters and discover the marine life at your leisure.

Sleeper Ride

Experience the thrilling sleeper ride on the water. An exciting and unique adventure that will keep you on your toes! Get towed behind a speedboat and hold on tight for an unforgettable rollercoaster-like experience on the waves.

Sofa Ride

Enjoy a comfortable yet thrilling water adventure! The Sofa Ride combines comfort with excitement as you float on an inflatable sofa-style platform towed behind a boat. A unique experience perfect for groups and families.

Banana Ride

Get ready for non-stop fun and laughter! The Banana Ride is a classic water adventure where you and your friends sit on an inflatable banana-shaped tube and get towed behind a speedboat. Perfect for group outings and creating unforgettable memories.

Double Banana Ride

For the ultimate group adventure! Two banana tubes towed simultaneously for double the fun and excitement. Perfect for large groups who want to challenge their friends to the water thrill games while enjoying the speed and waves together.

Band Wagon

Experience the largest group water ride available! The Band Wagon accommodates more participants than traditional tubes, making it ideal for extended friend groups and corporate events. Enjoy the thrill while your entire squad experiences the adventure together.

Speed Boat Ride

For the ultimate adrenaline junkies! Ride in a high-speed boat and experience the rush as you race across the water at maximum throttle. Feel the ocean breeze and the thrill of pure speed in this unforgettable adventure perfect for extreme sports enthusiasts.

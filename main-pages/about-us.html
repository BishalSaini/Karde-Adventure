The Soul of the Sea

Our Story & Mission

Born from a Passion for Ocean Adrenaline.

At Karde Beach Adventures, we are passionate about bringing the thrill of water sports to everyone. Founded with a vision to create unforgettable experiences on the serene Karde Beach in Dapoli, we have been serving adventure enthusiasts for over a decade.

Our journey began when we realized the untapped potential of Karde Beach as a premier water sports destination. With its calm, clean waters, we knew this was the perfect place to create magical moments for adventure seekers.

Today, we pride ourselves on delivering world-class water sports experiences while maintaining the highest safety standards and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the gold standard of adventure tourism in India.

Our Value

Safety first, excitement always, nature forever.

Karde Waves is Maharashtra's premier ocean adventure hub, bringing world-class water sports to the pristine shores of Dapoli.

Our Experiences

Explore our curated selection of water sports designed for every level of adventure.

Jet Ski

Experience the thrill of high-speed navigation across the waves. Our professional instructors guide you through the basics or let you take full control of our premium watercraft.

Speed Boat Rides

A perfect group activity. Feel the wind in your hair as we navigate the coastline at exhilarating speeds, showcasing the hidden beauty of Karde Beach.

Coastal Kayaking

For those seeking a more personal connection with the sea. Explore the calm morning waters and discover marine life at your own pace.

Sleeper Ride

Relaxed, comfortable long-distance boat ride with scenic views — perfect for sunset trips.

Sofa Ride

A fun inflatable sofa pulled by the boat — gentle, family-friendly splash ride.

Banana Ride

Classic inflatable banana boat for groups — lots of laughter and splashes.

Double Banana Ride

A larger banana for bigger groups — double the fun and stability.

Band Wagon

Large party float for groups — music, fun, and a social splash experience.

Karde Waves is Maharashtra's premier ocean adventure hub, bringing world-class water sports to the pristine shores of Dapoli.
